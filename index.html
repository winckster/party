Rachel's and Gabriel's
Celebration Weekend

Welcome/Willkommen

We're throwing a party! It's taking place May 20-22, 2016 at the Marin Headlands, which is just across the Golden Gate Bridge from San Francisco.

Wir schmeißen eine Party! Wir laden Euch ein, mit uns vom 20. bis 22. Mai 2015 in den Marin Headlands (direkt über die Golden Gate Bridge) zu feiern.

Accommodation/Unterbringung

We have rented the entire main house of the Marin Headlands Hostel for the weekend, and whether it's 5 miles or 5000 miles from your home, we would love to have you stay there as our guests. It is walking distance to the ocean, and is where we will be hosting meals and other merriment. The hostel is ours from Friday afternoon through Sunday morning.

Wir haben das gesamte Haupthaus des Marin Headlands Hostels für das Wochenende gemietet, und würden uns sehr freuen mit Euch das Wochenende zu verbringen. Der Strand ist nicht weit weg, und wir werden uns um Spiel, Speis und Trank bemühen.

Transportation/Anreise

By Bike: The Marin Headlands Hostel is a beautiful bike ride (~12 miles) from San Francisco or a gorgeous ferry trip (30 min) plus bike ride from Sausalito (~5 miles).

Mit dem Fahrrad: Am meisten Spaß macht die Anreise mit dem Fahrrad über die Golden Gate bridge (~ 19 Kilometer von San Francisco). Etwas bequemer ist es mit der Fähre (30 min) und dem Rad von Sausalito (~ 8 Kilometer).

By Bus: The Muni 76 bus comes to the Headlands from San Francisco only on Saturdays and Sundays.

Mit dem Bus: Der Muni 76 Bus färt nur an Samstagen und Sonntagen von San Francisco in die Headlands.

By Taxi: A taxi from downtown San Francisco will take approximately 25 minutes. If you're coming straight from the airport, check out Marin Door to Door.

Mit dem Taxi: Mit dem Taxi oder Uber / Lyft dauert es von der Innenstadt ca. 25 Minuten. Vom Flughafen gibt es einen Marin Door to Door shuttle.

By Car: It's about a 25 minute drive from downtown San Francisco. We encourage you to take the scenic route via Conzelman Road.

Mit dem Auto: Aus der Innenstad dauert es etwa 25 Minuten. Wir empfehlen Euch die landschaftlich schöne Strecke über Conzelman Road.

What to Bring/Was Zu Bringen

Come with attire for the beach, mellow hiking, and enthusiastic dancing. Linens and towels are provided by the hostel. Check this forecast and be prepared for sun, fog, large temperature differences between day and night, and possibly rain. Bringing items such as frisbees, musical instruments, kites and other games is strongly encouraged, especially for the locals. We also ask that you come with a willingness to assist with cooking, furniture rearrangement, etc. In fact, we want no gifts other than your help making this event happen.

Activites/Aktivitäten

On Saturday you should feel free to enjoy all the area has to offer. After packing a lunch (we'll provide the supplies, but please bring a water bottle), some of us plan to walk to the beach, meander over to a secluded cove near Bird Island, and then make our way to the Point Bonita Lighthouse. The tunnel to the lighthouse is only open from 12:30-3:30pm, and the area at the trailhead to the 1/2 mile walk down makes a great picnic location. We can return to the hostel by walking along Field Rd. The total loop is just under 4 miles. Note that there is parking at Rodeo Beach and at the lighthouse trailhead for anyone who would prefer to drive. There are plenty of variations or alternate nearby destinations and we encourage you to explore!

There is a large lawn in front of the hostel suitable for frisbee, badminton, or simple relaxation, and we will gather there at end of the afternoon for drinks and food, eventually moving inside for dinner, music and dancing. It also happens to be a full moon, so another trip outside might be in store.
