Rachel's and Gabriel's
Bay Area Celebration Weekend

Welcome

We're having a party! It's taking place May 20-22, 2016 at the Marin Headlands, which is just over the Golden Gate Bridge from San Francisco.

Accommodation

We have rented the entire main house of the Marin Headlands Hostel for the weekend, and we would love to have you stay there as our guests. Whether it's 5 miles or 5000 miles from your home, we hope you'll join us there from Friday evening through Sunday morning. It is walking distance to the ocean, and is where we will be hosting meals and other merriment.

Transportation

By Bike: The Marin Headlands Hostel is a beautiful bike ride (~12 miles) from San Francisco or a gorgeous ferry trip (30 min) plus bike ride from Sausalito (~5 miles).

By Bus: The Muni 76 bus comes to the Headlands from San Francisco only on Saturdays and Sundays.

By Taxi: A taxi or uber from downtown San Francisco will take approximately 25 minutes. If you're coming straight from the airport, check out Marin Door to Door.

By Car: It's about a 25 minute drive from downtown San Francisco. We encourage you to take the scenic route via Conzelman Road.

Activities

Stay tuned for more details about hiking, swimming, dancing, eating, drinking and enjoying all that the location has to offer!
